WordPress is the CMS choice. Purchase premium themes from [Themeforest](https://themeforest.net/) ($30-$70 one-time). Popular themes include Roxeen and Avada.

### Payment Services

The WooCommerce plugin handles payment processing with no setup fees. Use Stripe as your payment gateway provider.

### Printing and Drop Shipping

[Printful](https://www.printful.com/) provides on-demand printing and fulfillment. Here's how it works: customers pay your store, you pay Printful for production costs, and you keep the difference as profit. Printful handles printing and shipping directly to end customers.
